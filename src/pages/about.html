LDN Film Festival 2021

LDN FF is a film festival held every fall in London, presented by the Film at Lincoln Center (FLC). Founded in 1963 by Richard Roud and Amos Vogel with the support of Lincoln Center president William Schuman, it is one of the longest-running and most prestigious film festivals in the United States.[1] The non-competitive festival is centered on a “Main Slate” of typically 20–30 feature films, with additional sections for experimental cinema and new restorations.
